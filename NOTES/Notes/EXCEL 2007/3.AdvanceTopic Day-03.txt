GROUP AND UNGROUP:
====================
1.GROUP:
=========
GO TO DATA TAB-->NEXT GOTO OUTLINE TAB -->IN THAT SELECT GROUP 
AND CHOOSE ROW/COLUMN OPTIONS.

2.UNGROUP:
===========
GO TO DATA TAB-->NEXT GOTO OUTLINE TAB -->IN THAT SELECT UNGROUP 
AND CHOOSE ROW/COLUMN OPTIONS.

SUBTOTAL:
==========
GO TO DATA TAB-->NEXT GOTO OUTLINE TAB -->IN THAT SELECT SUBTOTAL.
